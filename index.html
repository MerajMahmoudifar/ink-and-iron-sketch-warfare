Commander Ranks & Progression

Advance through 7 Rank Tiers (Recruit → General). Promotions require meeting both Minimum Matches Played and Minimum Wins. Track live rank progress on your Profile tab.

Smart Auto-Stances & Badges

Units automatically adopt tactical stances with canvas badges:
DEFEND (+25% Defense) — On Bases, Capture Zones, or stationary.
AMBUSH (Stealth + 1.5x Dmg) — Restricted strictly to Infantry inside Forest tiles.
ADVANCE (Max Speed) — Moving along waypoints. (Manual override available in Inspector).

Faction Doctrines & Exclusive Units

Iron Corps: Heavy Armor (+20% defense on fortified tiles) & Heavy Siege Tank.
Vanguard Legion: Blitz Mobility (+1 speed on open ground) & Blitz Recon Car.

Command Power & Special Abilities

Earn CP by holding zones (+1/turn) and dealing damage (+1 per 25 dmg).
Flare (2 CP) — Reveals 3×3 fog for 1 turn.
Smoke (3 CP) — Blocks direct attacks in 3×3 area.
Artillery (4 CP) — 35 splash damage on 3×3 area.

Fog of War & Strict Recon

Shrouded tiles completely hide enemy units on the map and in the Inspector panel. Units inside Forests remain invisible until attacking or an enemy steps adjacent.

Victory Conditions & Siege Lockout

Victory: Capture enemy Main Base or eliminate all enemy units.
Under Siege: If an enemy is adjacent to your Base or Supply Zone, recruitment is locked until cleared.
