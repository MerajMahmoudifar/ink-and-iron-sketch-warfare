Quick-Start Commander Guide & FAQ

New to Sketch Warfare? Here is everything you need to know about commanding your army:

1. Recruit Troops Buy troops at your Base (Blue ring) from left store.
2. Draw Waypoints Click troop, then click adjacent tiles to draw paths.
3. Simultaneous Turns Press End Turn. 20s Planning ends & troops move!
4. Capture & Destroy Capture Gold Supply Zones (+10 Ink). Destroy AI Base (Red)!
Q: What do I do? Capture the enemy Main Base or eliminate all enemy troops to win.
Q: Where do I click? Click unit cards to buy; click troops then adjacent tiles to draw paths.
Q: Which tiles are mine vs enemy? Blue Ring = Your Base (Top-Left); Red Ring = AI Base (Bottom-Right); Gold = Neutral.
Q: How do I earn Ink? You earn +50 base Ink/turn + +10 bonus Ink/turn for each captured Supply Zone.
Q: Does it have rounds? Yes! 20-second Planning Phase (queue moves) followed by 3-second Playback.
Q: Do troops move by themselves? No! Troops follow your exact drawn waypoints and auto-attack enemies.

Commander Ranks & Progression

Advance through 7 Rank Tiers (Recruit → General). Promotions require meeting both Minimum Matches Played and Minimum Wins.

Smart Auto-Stances & Badges

DEFEND (+25% Defense) — On Bases, Capture Zones, or stationary.
AMBUSH (Stealth + 1.5x Dmg) — Restricted strictly to Infantry in Forests.
ADVANCE (Max Speed) — Moving along waypoints.

Faction Doctrines & Exclusive Units

Iron Corps: Heavy Armor (+20% defense on fortified tiles) & Heavy Siege Tank.
Vanguard Legion: Blitz Mobility (+1 speed on open ground) & Blitz Recon Car.

Command Power & Special Abilities

Flare (2 CP) — Reveals 3×3 fog for 1 turn.
Smoke (3 CP) — Neutral physical cloud. Blocks line-of-sight & direct attacks for 2 turns.
Artillery (4 CP) — 35 splash damage on 3×3 area.
