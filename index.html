Turn & Playback Structure

Each round: 20s Planning Phase (recruit, queue paths, use abilities) then a 3s Playback Phase (simultaneous movement and auto-combat).

Multi-Turn Waypoints

Draw long paths across the board. Blue = Turn 1 • Gold = Turn 2 • Purple = Turn 3+. Unconsumed waypoints carry over automatically.

Command Power & Abilities

Earn CP by holding zones (+1/turn) and dealing damage (+1 per 25 dmg).
Flare (2 CP) — Reveals 3×3 fog for 1 turn.
Smoke (3 CP) — Blocks attacks in 3×3 area.
Artillery (4 CP) — 35 splash damage on 3×3 zone.

Fog of War & Forest Stealth

Unrevealed tiles are hatched. Enemy units outside vision stay hidden. Troops in Forests stay invisible until they attack or an enemy steps adjacent.

Grid Blocking & Tie-Breakers

Enemies are impassable barriers. Only 1 unit per tile at turn end. Faster, more agile units claim contested tiles first.

Siege Lockout

If an enemy stands on or adjacent to your Base or Supply Zone it is Under Siege and cannot recruit until cleared.
